Welcome to Viking

We are a state-of-the-art gym that offers a wide range of fitness programs and martial arts classes that are designed to help you achieve your fitness goals while learning self-defense techniques.

Our Programs

At Viking, we believe that fitness and martial arts go hand in hand. Our experienced and certified trainers are passionate about helping you transform your body and mind through our diverse range of fitness programs, which include:

  • High-intensity interval training (HIIT)
  • Cardio

Our martial arts classes are taught by highly skilled and experienced instructors who specialize in various disciplines such as:

  • Muay Thai
  • MMA
  • Boxing

Join Our Community
